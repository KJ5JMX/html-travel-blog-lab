My time in Colorado

Colorado has always been a home away from home. The weather, people and the
enviroment are where I thrive. From offroading and camping to hikes to a simple
old school coffee shop in the middle of historic downtowns. I love it all.

Dispersed camping in Colorado

My wife and I went to Colorado last year to celebrate our 1 year anniversary. It was a blast but there were some scares along the way.

I'm not going to lie, it's a little intimidating to go camping outside the normal national and state parks. You loose your
sense of safty when it comes to the animals that are around. Always haveing to make sure all of your food is put away is KEY!
Our 3rd night there, we were laying in bed and thought that we heard something rustling through our camp.
It sounded JUST LIKE a bear. I promise. After a little time panicking, I was able to use my flashlight to determine
it was just some tarp flapping in the wind. I still just tell people that I am a bear attack surviror.
